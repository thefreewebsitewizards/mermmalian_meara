Meara the MerMammal

Underwater Festival Private Unique

Meara swims from the lost city of Atlantis. Now an urban ocean metropolis, it is filled with mermaids, creatures and hybrids alike in a sprawling city landscape quite similar to Detroit. Meara is a mammalian mermaid species of cetacean descent. Her name means "lake" honoring her Irish Bottlenose ancestry. She traveled from the depths of the Atlantic Ocean through the St. Lawrence Seaway all the way to the Clinton River where she now calls home.

Meara has many certificates in both water safety and first aid including CPR/AED, First Aid, SSI Open Water SCUBA, SSI Mermaid, SSI Ocean Mermaid and NAUI level 1.

Nellie the MerNymph

Festival Birthday Private

Nellie is a fresh water river nymph from the local watersheds that connect to the Detroit River. Originally manifested into being by old campfire stories from the fur traders along the Great Lakes - a mysterious woman spotted near the water, a flash of green, a splash, a huge fish tail, and you're second guessing yourself in the summer heat.

A smaller cousin to mermaids, this nymph prefers shallow, shady, streams and little lily ponds to the deep open waters of her mermaid cousins. Nellie's favorite basking spots are along the shores of Belle Isle. Nellie's past appearances cover all sorts of events, from tank appearances, to birthday parties, to lake swim ups, and dry set ups as well. Nellie is always enchanted to bring the magic to any occasion.
